Husky Game Dev

Who We Are

Husky Game Dev is a student organization aimed at supporting indie game development at the Bothell campus of the University of Washington. Our goal is to provide a community, educational opportunities, and resources for UWB students to learn skills related to game development and connect with industry professionals.

Events

Quarterly Game Jam

Every quarter we hold a game jam that gives students an incentive to get coding and developing games for fun! This is a perfect opportunity for you to form teams, brainstorm ideas, and make your game ideas come to life!

Showcases

Students have the opportunity to show off their game projects and view other student work at our end-of-quarter showcases. Showcase information is available on our Discord.

Workshops

Learn more about game development through our workshops! We will walk you through skills that will teach you how to be a game developer and designer.
