Husky Game Dev

Who We Are

Husky Game Dev is a student organization aimed at supporting indie game development at the Bothell campus of the University of Washington. Our goal is to provide a community, educational opportunities, and resources for UWB students to learn skills related to game development and connect with industry professionals.
