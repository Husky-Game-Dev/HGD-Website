Husky Game Dev

Who We Are

Husky Game Dev is a student organization aimed at supporting indie game development at the Bothell campus of the University of Washington. Our goal is to provide a community, educational opportunities, and resources for UWB students to learn skills related to game development and connect with industry professionals.

Game Jams

Every quarter, Husky Game Dev holds a game jam that gives students an incentive to get coding and developing games for fun! This is a perfect opportunity for you to form teams, brainstorm ideas, and make your game ideas come to life!

Spring Quarter Events
April 10, 2020
3:30pm - 5:30pm
2D Sprites and Sprite Sheets
Adrienne Co - Creative Director
Location: Discord
A workshop on how to make 2D sprites, use sprite sheets, and recommended programs to do so. We will also cover what dimensions you should use, spacing, importing them into Unity, and using them as proper sprites in Unity.
April 15, 2020
3:30pm - 5:30pm
2D Platformer Movement in Unity
Hao Duong - Technical Director
Location: Discord
A workshop on basic player / camera scripts in a 2D / platformer game. You will learn how to implement movement scripts (ground check, speed, jumping, sprite flips), platform collision, and camera following with screen clamping. Unity is required for this workshop.
April 24, 2020
4:30pm - 6:30pm
Introduction to Game Development
HGD x ACM Collab
Location: Zoom
The first of 3 collaborative workshops with UWB ACM..
Presented by HGD officers Vincent Allen, Adrienne Co, and Hao Duong, and ACM officers Jay Quedado and Cameron Ufland.
We will go over important roles and elements of game development, as well as resources and tools for students to begin creating their own games. Prior experience with creating games is not required!
May 15, 2020
4:30pm - 5:30pm
Game Development Processes
HGD x ACM Collab
Location: Zoom
The second of 3 collaborative workshops with UWB ACM.
Presented by HGD officers Vincent Allen, Adrienne Co, and Hao Duong, and ACM officers Jay Quedado and Cameron Ufland.
Learn how to set up a game development environment, plan the development of your game, and how to effectively collaborate with other people on a game.
May 22, 2020
4:30pm - 5:30pm
Game Engines
HGD x ACM Collab
Location: Zoom
The third of 3 collaborative workshops with UWB ACM.
Presented by HGD officers Vincent Allen, Adrienne Co, and Hao Duong, and ACM officers Jay Quedado and Cameron Ufland.
Learn about what a game engine is, the differences between common game engines like Unity, RPGMaker, and Godot, and how to choose a game engine for a game development project.
